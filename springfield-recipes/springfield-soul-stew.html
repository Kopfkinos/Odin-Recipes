Springfield Soul Stew

Description

Um, today's special is Springfield Soul Stew. We sell so much of this, people wonder what we put in it. So, we're gonna tell ya right now.

Ingredients

  • half a teacup of bass
  • 1 pound of fatback drums
  • 4 tablespoons of boilin' Springfield guitar
  • just a little pinch of organ
  • half a pint of horn

Steps

  1. Mix the bass and drums in a bowl
  2. Gently mix in each tablespoon of guitar in one at a time, until mixture is smooth and groovy
  3. Throw in that pinch of organ like your life depends on it
  4. Pour in the horn all in one fell swoop and now mix until a beautiful colour
  5. Place on the burner and bring to a boil
